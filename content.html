Design Projects

Book Cover

I redesigned a book cover for an existing book. I chose to redesign the cover for the book Six Crimson Cranes by Elizabeth Lim. I wanted to give the cover a more serious tone than the original so I went with a black, red, and white color scheme. I first started out making illustrations for the front and back in Adobe Fresco. I then went into Indesign and added a red border along with text to contrast with the black and white illustrations. Overall, I really enjoyed taking a light-hearted fantasy novel and redesigning the cover so it had a darker and more mature vibe.

  • Photoshop
  • Fresco
  • InDesign

Tesla Annual Report

I designed a booklet for Tesla based on their 2023 annual shareholder report. For the design, I wanted to focus on the aspect of Tesla being a luxury vehicle. Instead of going for a friendlier tone, I went with a dark and cold theme. I wanted the brand to feel more high-end and exclusive for this report booklet.

  • Photoshop
  • InDesign

Mobile App

The goal was to use research to create a design solution for a problem of our choice. I chose to design an app that simplifies the research process for finding trails. I designed an app that caters to outdoor enthusiasts and athletes. For the design, I went with a natural yet energetic tone, incorporating colors like bright orange and dark greens.

  • Illustrator
